Your daily practice

I
Morning

Set Your Intention

Goals for the day, three gratitudes, a personal affirmation, and reflection on an athlete-curated quote. Five minutes that frame the next sixteen hours.

II
Post-Workout

Reflect on the Session

Rate your feeling, energy, and focus. Note what clicked and what didn't. Auto-imported from Whoop or logged manually. The freshest insights come right after training.

III
Evening

Close the Day

How did the day go? What applied from your morning quote? Overall mood and honest self-assessment. The loop closes, the data builds, the patterns emerge.

IV
Continuous

AI Connects the Dots

Over time, Daily Ritual reveals relationships between your mental state and physical performance that no single entry could show you.

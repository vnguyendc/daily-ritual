Your daily practice

I
Morning

Set Your Intention

Goals for the day, three gratitudes, a personal affirmation, and reflection on an athlete-curated quote. Five minutes that frame the next sixteen hours.

II
Post-Workout

Reflect on the Session

Rate your feeling, energy, and focus. Note what clicked and what didn't. The freshest insights come right after training.

III
Evening

Close the Day

How did the day go? Overall mood and honest self-assessment. The loop closes, the data builds, the patterns emerge.
